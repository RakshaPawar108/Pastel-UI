Images

Images are elements added on websites to make them engaging to the users. They are great for displaying ideas and content in visual format.

Round Image

You can get round image by adding the img-round class to your img tag.

Image with Rounded corners

You can get image with rounded corners by adding the img-rounded-corner class to your img tag.

Responsive Image

You can get a responsive image by adding the img-responsive class to the img tag.
